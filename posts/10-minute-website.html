Creating your own basic .com website in 10 minutes

  1. Go to godaddy.com and register a domain for a few years, with Whois protection enabled.
  2. Go to wordpress.com and make a free website, like mysite.wordpress.com.
  3. In GoDaddy, set up permanent forwarding from "mysite.com" (or whatever the domain is that you paid for) to "mysite.wordpress.com", with domain masking, by following this tutorial: https://www.godaddy.com/help/forward-a-domain-12123 (you can do this in under 2 minutes).

Optional: upgrade your WordPress to premium so it removes the "Blog at WordPress.com." stuff at the bottom of your page.

After waiting 2 minutes-2 days, you'll be able to go to mysite.com and get instantly redirected to mysite.wordpress.com. When you edit your site via WordPress, mysite.com will get updated too, almost instantly.
